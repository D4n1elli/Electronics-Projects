# Windmill Project
This project was made on 17/04/2026 and committed 2 days later

## How I made it
First, I built only the wood body of the windmill, using sticks and silicon glue, then, made the propeller and placed it on the roof base. At first, I didn't intend to make it work with a motor, it only had a crank behind it so you could spin the propeller itself. But 2 days ago I was analyzing a DC motor I have and decided to try to use it in the windmill. I then soldered the DC motor to two wires and a push-button and glued it to the propeller.

## How it works
By pressing the button on the lower part of the windmill, the propeller rotates like a real windmill.
When the push-button is pressed, it completes the circuit and activates the DC motor

## Materials used
- Wood sticks
- Silicon glue and instant glue
- Wires
- A push-button
- DC motor
- Arduino or battery
